Pixel Heritage: The Retro Future of Amiga Demonscene

The demoscene originated in the 1980s from the practice of software crackers adding their own "cracktros" or intro screens to pirated games, which eventually evolved into a distinct creative community. Demosceners compete to create the most technically impressive and visually/aurally stunning demos, often under strict size constraints like 64KB or 4KB, to push the limits of what can be achieved on a given hardware platform.
