Glossary of Terms

 * Neural Light Computing: NVIDIA's patented technology using neural networks to manipulate light for AR applications.
 * Spatial Light Modulator (SLM): A device used to modulate amplitude, phase, or polarization of light waves in space and time.
 * Adaptive Intelligence: The ability of a system to learn and adapt to its environment and usage patterns.
 * Optical Occlusion: The blocking or altering of light paths to create the illusion of solid virtual objects in AR.
 * AndroidXR: Google's open platform initiative for AR and VR development.
 * Edge AI: Artificial intelligence algorithms processed locally on a device rather than in the cloud.
 * Neural Adaptation: The process by which neural networks adjust to compensate for imperfections or changes in their environment.
