Generative AI Funding Surges

Generative AI funding has surged to $56 billion across 885 deals in 2024, a 192% increase from the previous year. Major players like OpenAI and Anthropic are spearheading investments, with U.S. companies leading the way, although international startups are also making significant contributions.
