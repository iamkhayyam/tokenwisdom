# Meta Doubles Down on AGI

Meta's Plan and the Power of Its Behavioral Data





The Power of Behavioral Data

Meta's commitment to artificial general intelligence (AGI) opens up a world of possibilities for technological innovation. However, it is crucial to examine the implications of Meta's vast collection of behavioral data on Facebook users. This paper delves into the intricate relationship between AGI, behavioral data, and the potential for a groundbreaking method of pre-suasion.


The Potential of AGI and Pre-suasion

In today's digital age, behavioral data available on Facebook has become an invaluable resource. Every click, like, and share provides insights into our preferences, desires, and even thought processes. Meta, with its wealth of data, has an unmatched advantage in understanding human behavior. This advantage can be harnessed to develop an AGI system that goes beyond predictions, delving into mind-reading capabilities.


Benefits and Applications

By integrating behavioral data into an AGI system, Meta can explore new possibilities for pre-suasion. Pre-suasion, a concept coined by social psychologist Robert Cialdini, involves subtly influencing someone's mindset before a decision is made. Leveraging behavioral data, Meta could create an AGI system capable of understanding individual preferences, biases, and emotional states. This system could then tailor persuasive messages in a way that deeply resonates with each user, leading to more successful persuasion.


Ethical Considerations: Privacy, Consent, and Manipulation

The potential benefits of such a system are vast. From marketing to political campaigns, the ability to pre-suade individuals in a highly personalized manner could revolutionize the way messages are conveyed. By tapping into the intricacies of human behavior, an AGI system developed by Meta can optimize strategies for influence, resulting in more effective communication and greater impact.


Addressing Inequality and Concentration of Power

However, it is essential to address the ethical considerations surrounding the utilization of behavioral data and pre-suasion techniques. Consent, privacy, and the potential for manipulation cannot be ignored. Meta must prioritize utmost transparency and user control over their data while establishing ethical boundaries to prevent misuse. Striking a balance between innovation and responsible data usage is crucial to avoid crossing ethical lines.


Risks and Unintended Consequences

Meta's access to vast amounts of behavioral data raises concerns about the concentration of power and influence, potentially exacerbating existing inequalities. If the ability to pre-suade individuals becomes concentrated in the hands of a few dominant entities, it could further consolidate wealth and influence. To mitigate this risk, Meta must prioritize inclusivity, collaboration, and open access, ensuring that the benefits of AGI and pre-suasion are distributed equitably and contribute to a more just and equitable society.


Establishing Ethical Frameworks for AGI

Additionally, Meta must consider the potential risks and unintended consequences that may arise from the development of an AGI system intertwined with behavioral data. As AGI becomes more autonomous and capable of independent decision-making, questions of accountability and transparency become paramount. It is critical for Meta to establish a robust ethical framework, incorporating guidelines for responsible development and deployment of AGI technologies. Collaboration with policymakers, researchers, and other stakeholders is essential to create comprehensive governance and regulatory frameworks.


Token Wisdom

Meta's focus on AGI opens up new possibilities for technological advancement. However, the integration of behavioral data and the development of pre-suasion within an AGI system require careful consideration. With access to vast amounts of behavioral data, Meta has the potential to create a groundbreaking system that understands and influences human behavior. Yet, it is crucial for Meta to navigate the ethical challenges of privacy, consent, and equitable distribution of power. By fostering transparency, collaboration, and responsible development, Meta can ensure that AGI serves as a force for positive change while respecting the values and well-being of its users.
