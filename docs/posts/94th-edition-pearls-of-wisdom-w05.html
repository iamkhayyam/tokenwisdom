Technical Terms:

  • Quantum Computing: A type of computing that uses quantum-mechanical phenomena like superposition and entanglement to perform operations on data.
  • AI Jailbreak: An attempt to bypass an AI system's ethical constraints or programmed limitations to make it perform unintended or potentially harmful actions.
  • Open-Source AI: Artificial intelligence models and tools whose code is publicly available for use, modification, and distribution.
  • Language Model Scaling: The process of increasing the size and complexity of AI language models to improve their performance and capabilities.
  • Venture Capital: A form of private equity financing provided by firms or funds to startups and small companies believed to have long-term growth potential.
  • Red Teaming: A practice in cybersecurity where a group attempts to breach a system's defenses to test its security measures.
  • RPG (Role-Playing Game): A game genre where players control the actions of characters in a fictional setting, often with complex narratives and character development.
  • Chip Design: The process of creating the layout and architecture of integrated circuits used in electronic devices.
