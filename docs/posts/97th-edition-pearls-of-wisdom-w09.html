Technical Terms:

  • Holographic Capture: A technique for recording three-dimensional information about a scene.
  • Quantum Sensors: Devices that use quantum mechanical effects to measure physical quantities with high precision.
  • Terahertz Waves: Electromagnetic waves with frequencies between those of microwaves and infrared light.
  • Hive-Mind AI: A type of artificial intelligence that mimics collective intelligence found in nature.
  • Open-Source Framework: Software tools and libraries made freely available for modification and distribution.
  • Light-Based Computing: Computation using photons instead of electrons, potentially offering higher speeds and efficiency.
  • Circuit Board Fabrication: The process of creating printed circuit boards for electronic devices.
  • Quantum Computing: A type of computation that uses quantum-mechanical phenomena for processing information.
  • Cryptocurrency: Digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security.
  • Coherent Light: Light waves that are in phase, typically produced by lasers.
