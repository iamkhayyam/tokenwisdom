Explosive Growth of AI: From Chat GPT to AGI Sparks

By 🌶️ @iamkhayyam
April 01, 2023 · 2 min read
A.I. (and Subsets)ANI \ AGI \ ASILLMs - Language ModelsOpenAI + e/acc

Artificial intelligence is booming 🔮

It took a major leap forward with Chat GPT's release, revolutionizing education and copy editing. This ignited a "tech war" between Google and Microsoft, each integrating AI into their search engines. Open AI then revealed the next generation of their language model, GPT4, which can recognize images and left users dumbfounded once again. Microsoft researchers believe GPT4 is exhibiting "sparks of AGI" - something that was not anticipated for two more decades. This article examines the recent progress in AI and the implications of these AGI sparks.

AI Wars: Google and Microsoft

Google and Microsoft are fiercely competing in AI development. Google released Chat GPT, which disrupted education and copy editing. Microsoft unveiled plans to incorporate the technology into their search engine, setting off a rivalry between them and their competitor. GPT4 was introduced, leaving users astonished. However, Microsoft researchers revealed that GPT4 is showing "sparks of artificial general intelligence (AGI)," which is a feat that was not expected to happen for the next 20 years.

The Next Generation of Language Model: GPT4

Open AI revealed the next generation of their language model, GPT4. This new model is smarter than ever before and can recognize images. It left users dumbfounded once again, and the internet was flooded with incredible use cases. The new GPT model has expanded the capabilities of its text generation AI. New applications of the technology were being discovered every day, and even its creators had no idea what it could do or the precise means of how it does it. The implications of this are enormous, and many are left wondering what industries are going to remain untouched at this rate.

AGI Sparks and the Implications

Microsoft researchers admit that GPT4 is showing "sparks of artificial general intelligence (AGI)." AGI is the ability of AI systems to achieve intellectual tasks that humans can, which wasn't expected for another two decades. However, GPT4 demonstrates it's now a reality. AGI could revolutionize medicine, education, and transportation, yet also cause job losses and social disruptions.

Token Wisdom

The explosive growth of AI is now more evident than ever. With the release of Chat GPT and GPT4, education and copy editing were disrupted, and the next generation of language models has expanded the capabilities of text generation AI. The potential effects that AGI has on society are vast and varied. We must ensure that the development of AGI takes proper precautions to maximize the benefits and minimize any potential damage. If AI is doing all the thinking, what are we doing?

Something to think about.
