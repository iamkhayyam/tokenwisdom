Technical Terms:

  • Meme Coins - Cryptocurrencies created as a joke or based on internet memes, often with high volatility.
  • Stealth Mode - A startup strategy where a company operates in secrecy to avoid competition or attention.
  • AI Rollups - A technique that combines multiple AI models or processes to enhance efficiency and capabilities.
  • Public Domain - The state of creative works no longer protected by intellectual property laws, freely available for public use.
  • Semiconductor - A material with electrical conductivity between that of a conductor and an insulator, crucial for electronic devices.
  • Neurodiversity - The concept that neurological differences are natural variations in the human genome, not deficits to be cured.
  • User Interface (UI) - The space where interactions between humans and machines occur in device operation.
  • User Experience (UX) - The overall experience of a person using a product, especially in terms of how easy or pleasing it is to use.
